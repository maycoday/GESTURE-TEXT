Gesture Guide

Here are all the gestures currently integrated into GestureText. Make sure your hands are clearly visible and well-lit for best results.

Hello (Open Palm)

How to perform: Show your open palm with all fingers extended

Output: "Hello (Open Palm)"

Thumbs Up

How to perform: Extend only your thumb upward, keep other fingers folded

Output: "Thumbs Up"

Fist

How to perform: Close all fingers into a tight fist

Output: "Fist"

Point

How to perform: Extend only your index finger, keep others folded

Output: "Point"

Victory (V)

How to perform: Extend index and middle fingers in a V shape

Output: "Victory (V)"
